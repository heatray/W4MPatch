================================== W4M.Loader ==================================

Place modified XML, XOM or TGA files here from the following paths and they will
be loaded by the game instead of original ones:

- Data
- Data/Themes
- Data/Effects
- Data/Effects/ParticleData
- Data/Frontend/Levels
- Data/Tweak
- Data/databanks
- Data/Audio
- Data/Language/PC
- Data/scripts
- Data/Logos
- Data/Maps
- Data/HUD/Flags
- Data/HUD/Weapons
- Data/Objects
- Data/Temp
- Data/HUD
- Data/Character
- Data/Misc Objects
- Data/Weapons
- Data/Weapons/Custom
- Data/Frontend/Mechanics
- Data/Frontend/Icons
